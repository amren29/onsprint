Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the credit will be applied to your next billing cycle.

On the Starter plan, you can list up to 20 products. On the Growth plan, up to 50 products. If you need more, upgrade to the Pro plan for unlimited products. Your existing products will always remain accessible.

The platform fee is charged per successful transaction through your online store. It's separate from payment gateway fees (Stripe/Billplz). The fee decreases as you upgrade to higher plans. Free plan users don't have an online store, so no platform fee applies.

Yes, payment gateways (Stripe, Billplz, FPX) have their own transaction fees which are separate from our platform fee. We don't charge anything extra on top of their fees.

No long-term contracts. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime. Annual plans are billed upfront for the year and include 2 months free. You can cancel before renewal.

The Free plan includes up to 50 orders per month, the full production board with 12 stages, online proofing with Onsprint watermark, automatic production status updates, up to 30 customers, invoice generation, and basic reports. It's a fully functional order management system to get you started — just without the online store, stock management, or data export.
